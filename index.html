The Perceptron: Foundation of Neural Networks

Imagine you're building a robot that needs to make simple decisions - like whether to grab an object or not. How would you teach it to make these decisions? This is where our journey into perceptrons begins.

Just as we learned about biological neurons in our last lesson, today we're going to meet their simplest artificial cousin - the perceptron. It's the 'Hello World' of neural networks, and while it might seem basic at first, it contains all the fundamental ideas that power even the most sophisticated AI systems today.
